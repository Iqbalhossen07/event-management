Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about EventFlow and event management

How do I create my first event?

Creating an event is simple! Sign up, click 'Create Event', fill in your event details, customize your registration page, and publish. Our step-by-step wizard guides you through the entire process.

Can I sell tickets through EventFlow?

Yes! EventFlow includes a complete ticketing system with secure payment processing, multiple payment methods, discount codes, and real-time sales tracking.

Is live streaming included in all plans?

Live streaming is available on our Professional and Enterprise plans. It includes HD streaming, interactive chat, Q&A sessions, and recording capabilities.

How does the mobile app work?

Our mobile app allows attendees to view schedules, network with other participants, receive real-time updates, access event materials, and provide feedback seamlessly.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 customer support via chat, email, and phone. Enterprise customers also get dedicated account managers and technical support.

Can I customize the event branding?

Absolutely! You can fully customize colors, logos, fonts, and layouts to match your brand. White-label options are available for Enterprise plans.

Still have questions? Our support team is here to help!
