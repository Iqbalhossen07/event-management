Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our events and platform.

How do I register for an event?

Simply browse our events, select the one you're interested in, and click 'Register Now'. You'll be guided through our secure payment process.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Yes, we offer full refunds up to 48 hours before the event starts. Check individual event policies for specific terms.

Are events available for remote attendance?

Many of our events offer live streaming options for remote participants. Look for the 'Live Streaming' badge on event listings.

Do you provide certificates of attendance?

Yes, all registered attendees receive digital certificates of attendance that can be shared on professional networks.
